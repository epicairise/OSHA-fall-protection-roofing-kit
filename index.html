Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a roofing safety kit OSHA-compliant?
It must include fall arrest systems like a full-body harness, anchor, and lifeline, and meet OSHA 1926.502 standards.
Can I reuse fall protection gear after a job?
Anchors and some lifelines may be reusable, but full-body harnesses must be inspected for wear or damage before each use. After a fall, most gear should be retired immediately.
