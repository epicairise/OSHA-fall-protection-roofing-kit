Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a roofing safety kit OSHA-compliant?

It must include fall arrest systems like a full-body harness, anchor, and lifeline, and meet OSHA 1926.502 standards.

Can I reuse roofing safety kits?

Some components like anchors may be reusable, but always inspect equipment and follow manufacturer guidelines.

Are fall protection kits required for residential roofing?

Yes, OSHA requires fall protection at 6 feet or higher on residential jobs.
