Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a roofing safety kit OSHA-compliant?

It must include fall arrest systems like a full-body harness, anchor, and lifeline, and meet OSHA 1926.502 standards.

Can I reuse fall protection gear after a job?

Anchors and some lifelines may be reusable, but full-body harnesses must be inspected before each use. After a fall, most gear should be retired immediately.

What's the best fall protection setup for high-pitch residential roofs?

Mid-tier or pro-grade kits like Malta Dynamics or 3M DBI-SALA offer better comfort and grip features for angled roofs.

Do I need fall protection for one-story roofing jobs?

Yes. OSHA requires fall protection at elevations of 6 feet or higher ‚Äî even on single-story homes.
