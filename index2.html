Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a roofing safety kit OSHA-compliant?

It must include a full-body harness, lifeline, and anchor system, and meet the standards outlined in OSHA 1926.502 for fall protection.

Can I reuse fall protection gear after a job?

Anchors and some lifelines may be reusable, but full-body harnesses must be inspected for wear or damage before each use. After a fall, most gear should be retired immediately.

What‚Äôs the best fall protection setup for high-pitch residential roofs?

Mid-tier or pro-grade kits like Malta Dynamics or 3M DBI-SALA offer better comfort and grip features for steep, angled roofs.

Do I need fall protection for one-story roofing jobs?

Yes. OSHA requires fall protection at elevations of 6 feet or higher in the construction industry ‚Äî even for single-story residential projects.
